# IndonesiaCommercialProperty.com

Income-focused real estate platform offering stabilized assets with cash flow from day one.
No speculative development. No yield assumptions detached from reality.

---

## Investment Philosophy

We focus on **certainty of income**, not projected upside.

* Acquire or structure assets that are already income-generating
* Prioritize **cash yield over capital appreciation narratives**
* Eliminate development risk and lease-up speculation
* Target assets where value is created through **execution, not timing**

---

## What We Offer

Curated commercial and hybrid residential assets across Indonesia:

* Coliving properties (stabilized or near-stabilized)
* Ruko / shop-house income assets
* Small-format commercial buildings
* Repositioned properties with proven rental demand

Each asset is structured to produce **immediate rental income upon acquisition**.

---

## Underwriting Principles

* Real, in-place or contractually secured income
* Conservative expense assumptions
* No reliance on aggressive exit cap compression
* Focus on **downside protection first**, yield second

We avoid:

* Ground-up development risk
* Long vacancy assumptions
* “Pro forma-driven” deals without operational proof

---

## Typical Deal Profile

* Entry Yield: 7–10%
* Occupancy: Stabilized or with clear path to stabilization
* Rental Growth: Organic, market-driven (not engineered)
* Exit Strategy: Yield compression + income growth

## Case Study (Representative)

**Asset Type:** CoLiving SLB Asset

* Yield starts at 7% at years 1-3, and increases at year 4-10.
* Operator sells you the asset, lease it back from you, operate, and you earn passive income.
* Operator gives a 1.3x buyback guarantee.
* Starting yield, yield escalation, and exit capital gain is contractually guaranteed.
* Any downside is operator loss, any upside is operator promote fee. Risk stands on Operator side. Buyer have zero operational risk.
